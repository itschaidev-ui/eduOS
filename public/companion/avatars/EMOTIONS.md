## Companion emotion variants

If you want the companion to “react” (smug, blush, etc.), add optional images here:

- `public/companion/avatars/neutral.png` (fallback)
- `public/companion/avatars/smug.png`
- `public/companion/avatars/blush.png`
- `public/companion/avatars/happy.png`
- `public/companion/avatars/annoyed.png`

Recommended: transparent PNG, half-body crop.

If any are missing, the app falls back to `FullBodyNoBgUpscaled.png` (cropped to the face area).
